The Solution

SENTERNET TECHNOLOGIES designed a responsive, mobile-first web application focused on solving these core challenges. Explore the core features:

High-Resolution Portfolio Gallery

A clean, image-centric design was implemented to showcase separate, filterable galleries for Bridal, Arabic, and Simple designs. This allowed users to filter and view the artist's range of expertise instantly, directly addressing the portfolio fragmentation problem.

Integrated Service & Pricing Tiers

Clear, upfront pricing packages (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Bridal Premier) were displayed, detailing coverage, time taken, and cost. This transparency minimized back-and-forth price negotiations and empowered clients to make informed decisions.

Streamlined Booking Flow

A user-friendly, single-step booking request form was integrated. It requires only the essential details (date, occasion, package), enabling faster lead qualification and replacing the inefficient DM-based system entirely.

Trust & Transparency Modules

Dedicated sections were created to address client concerns upfront. This included a guarantee of 100% natural, chemical-free henna with detailed aftercare instructions, and a prominent display of client testimonials to build immediate social proof.
